Mojito Glass to use
category Type
Ingredient
  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • ½ medium lime, cut into 3 wedges, divided
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 cup ice cubes, or as needed
  • 1 ½ fluid ounces white rum
  • ½ cup club soda, or as needed
Instruction
  • STEP 1
    Muddle the lime juice, sugar and mint leaves in a
    small jug, crushing the mint as you go – you can
    use the end of a rolling pin for this.
    Pour into a tall glass and add a handful of ice.
  • STEP 2
    Pour over the rum, stirring with a long-handled
    spoon. Top up with soda water, garnish with mint
    and serve.
